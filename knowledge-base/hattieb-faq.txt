Hattie B's Hot Chicken - Frequently Asked Questions

WAIT TIMES
- Tourist/High-Traffic Locations (Lower Broad, Midtown): Peak hours 60-120 minutes
- Suburban Locations (Franklin, West Nashville): Peak hours 20-45 minutes
- Airport Location (BNA): 5-15 minutes, fastest service
- Best times to visit: Weekday afternoons 2-5 PM

ORDERING AHEAD
- Mobile App/Website ordering available up to 2 weeks in advance
- Use dedicated pickup counter to skip dine-in line
- IMPORTANT: Online orders must be consumed off-site (no dine-in with online orders)
- No reservations - strictly first-come, first-served
- Exception: Private dining rooms at Nashville Midtown (50 people) and Atlanta West Midtown (40 people)

ALLERGEN & DIETARY INFO
- Gluten-Free: Grilled chicken tenders available (NOT at Las Vegas), Naked chicken (fried without breading)
- Fries share fryer space - potential cross-contamination
- Garlic/Onion: High cross-contamination risk in spice blends
- No nuts in recipes but cannot guarantee vendor facility safety
- Soy and Egg present in breader and various sides

COOP CLUB LOYALTY PROGRAM
- Earn 10 points per $1 spent
- 1000 points = $10 reward (automatically converted)
- Points expire 12 months from earning date
- CANNOT use with DoorDash or third-party delivery orders
- Cash purchases: Upload receipt manually through app

CATERING
- Small Groups (Under 15): Use mobile app "Group Order" feature, 24 hours minimum notice
- Large Groups (15+): Email events@hattieb.com, 48-72 hours recommended notice
- Full-Service Events (100+): $2,500 minimum, 2 weeks minimum notice
- Delivery minimum: $300 food and beverage
- Nashville: $25 flat delivery fee within 10-mile radius

GIFT CARDS
- Available in-store and online at hattieb.com
- CANNOT use at Las Vegas location or BNA Airport
- No expiration, treated like cash - no replacement if lost
