Hattie B's Hot Chicken - Menu & Products

HEAT LEVELS (6 total, from mild to extreme)
1. Southern - No heat, family-friendly, all flavor
2. Mild - Gentle warmth, great for first-timers
3. Medium - Classic Nashville hot chicken, most popular choice
4. Hot - Significant heat, for spice enthusiasts
5. Damn Hot - Serious heat territory, not for beginners
6. Shut the Cluck Up - Extreme heat, challenge level, for heat seekers only

PROTEIN OPTIONS
- Chicken Tenders: Available in all heat levels, fried or grilled
- Half Bird: Traditional Nashville hot chicken presentation
- Wings: Jumbo hot chicken wings
- Naked Chicken: Fried without breading (gluten-free option)
- Grilled Option: Available at most locations (NOT at Las Vegas)

DIETARY ACCOMMODATIONS
Gluten-Free:
- Grilled chicken tenders (most locations, NOT Las Vegas)
- Naked chicken (fried without breading)
- Gluten-free spice blends upon request
- Multiple sides marked as gluten-free

Allergen Info:
- Fries share fryer space (cross-contamination possible)
- Garlic/onion in spice blends (cross-contamination risk)
- Soy and egg in breader
- No nuts in recipes (vendor safety not guaranteed)
- Staff trained on allergens - call ahead for severe allergies
